Projects > Games > Snake

Rules

  • Use the directional arrows to control the Snake's direction.
    Going backwards is not possible (e.g pressing Down while going upwards)

  • Eat food (blue-ish) to grow

  • Avoid poison (red) and own tail.
    The world is open but beware of the walls (black).

  • A portal is granted each 5 completed levels.
    Press the Spacebar to place the portal (two steps)
